Hot Chocolate Drink

Prep time:

20mins

Serves::

4

Calories:

800 Kcal/serving

Nutritional Information

Protein

8g

Carbohydrate

67g

Fat

6g

Sugar

15g

Fiber

4g

Description

Nasi Goreng is the popular Indonesian fried rice which is traditionally served with a fried egg. I love the unique dark brown, caramelised colour of the rice! It’s a simple recipe, you won’t need to hunt down any unusual ingredients, and it’s one of my favourite Indonesian foods – and I’m betting you will love it too.

Ingredients

2 cups of milk

1/2 cup of cocoa powder

2 tablespoons of sugar (or to taste)

1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

A pinch of salt

Whipped cream (optional)

Grated chocolate (optional)

Cooking Directions

1.

In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it begins to boil. Stir occasionally to prevent the milk from sticking to the bottom of the pan.

2.

Add the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt to the hot milk. Stir well until the chocolate and sugar are completely dissolved.

3.

Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the hot chocolate cook for another 2 to 3 minutes, stirring constantly. This will help slightly thicken the drink.

4.

Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the vanilla extract. Mix well.

5.

Pour the hot chocolate into cups or mugs. If desired, add whipped cream on top for decoration and sprinkle with grated chocolate.

6.

Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious hot chocolate!
